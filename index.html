The Core Problem

We're selling. We're not onboarding fast enough. Feb target was 6 PSA deals onboarded — we hit 4. The bottleneck isn't leads or closes. It's onboarding velocity. Solve this and the entire revenue engine accelerates.

The Four Initiatives
Initiative 01
⚙️

Automated Onboarding via Sales Intelligence

"Your system is 80% configured before your first call."
  • Sales process captures everything: workflows, integrations, use cases, pain points
  • At deal close, that data auto-configures the Rev.io instance — 50–80% built before Day 1
  • Remaining gaps filled via agent-guided client intake on kickoff
  • SA capacity doubles without adding headcount
🎯 Key metric: Days from close to fully onboarded — cut in half
Initiative 02
🔄

Feedback Loop: Client → Product → Marketing

Nothing gets lost. Every gap becomes a signal.
  • Onboarding friction flags immediately route to product team
  • Product roadmap updates trigger automatic marketing content generation
  • Market the "80% configured" story as a core differentiator — in real time
  • Full loop: Client Experience → Product Priority → Marketing Message → Next Sale
🎯 Key metric: Time from client feedback to marketing publish — days, not quarters
Initiative 03
🎯

Agentic Marketing — 1:1 at Scale

SaaS marketing was spray-and-pray. This is a sniper at scale.
  • Old model: blast campaigns to broad segments
  • New model: every prospect gets bespoke outreach, timed and personalized by agent
  • Cross-reference Apollo signals → when a lead re-engages, an agent fires instantly
  • Human reviews, approves, sends — agent does the heavy lifting
🎯 Key metric: Response rate on outreach vs. previous campaign benchmarks
Initiative 04
💀

Attack the Graveyard — ~$100K Opportunity

Canceled, on-hold, and pending deals aren't dead — they're waiting.
  • ~$100K sitting in canceled / on-hold / pending in Salesforce right now
  • Deploy agents to monitor each deal: company signals, personnel changes, competitor pain
  • Trigger fires → agent builds bespoke, contextual outreach → human reviews and sends
  • Cross-reference Apollo timing to know exactly when the window opens
🎯 Key metric: $$ recovered from graveyard pipeline in 90 days
